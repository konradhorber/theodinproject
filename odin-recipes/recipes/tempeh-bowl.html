Tempeh Bowl

Ingredients

  • Tempeh
  • Tempeh seasoning:
    • Sesame oil
    • Soy sauce
    • Honey/maple syrup
    • Garlic powder
  • Greens: Spinach/kale
  • Rice
  • Hummus

Steps

  1. Fry tempeh
  2. Add seasoning mid frying
  3. Fry green in other pan/pot
  4. Add rice to plate
  5. Serve greens and tempeh to plate
  6. Add hummus to plate
  7. Eat 👨 🍳
